# Privacy Policy for Contractor Pro

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Contractor Pro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our web application and services (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically through your use of the Service, and information we obtain from third-party sources.

### A. Information You Provide Directly

*   **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
*   **Business Data:** Information related to your business operations, including client names, contact details, project details, estimates, invoices, and payment records.
*   **Payment Information:** We do not store full payment card details. Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor, Stripe. We collect and store a tokenized version of your payment information.

### B. Information Collected Automatically

*   **Usage Data:** Information about how you access and use the Service, such as access times, pages viewed, and features used.
*   **Device Information:** Information about the device you use to access the Service, including the hardware model, operating system, and IP address.
*   **Location Information:** We may collect general location information (e.g., IP address) for features like employee tracking and route optimization.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   **Service Provision:** To provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including processing transactions, managing your business data, and facilitating communication.
*   **Personalization:** To personalize your experience, such as providing AI-powered suggestions and insights relevant to your business data.
*   **Communication:** To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
*   **Security and Fraud Prevention:** To protect the Service and our users from fraudulent or illegal activities.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information as follows:

*   **With Service Providers:** We share information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to perform services on our behalf.
    *   **Stripe:** For secure payment processing.
    *   **AI/LLM Providers (e.g., OpenAI):** For the AI-Powered Insights features.
    *   **Analytics Providers:** For monitoring and analyzing usage of the Service.
*   **Legal Compliance:** If we believe disclosure is required by any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
